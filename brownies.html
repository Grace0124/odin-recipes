Brownies

Description

This brownie recipe is fudgy and delicious. It uses protein powder and diet coke to keep it low-fat and protein-packed. Great for a late-night snack or a way to satisfy those chocolate cravings.

Ingredients

  • 63g cocoa powder
  • 35g protein powder
  • 45g oat flour
  • 100g erythritol
  • 3g baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 300g diet coke

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl. Using a sieve is recommended
  3. Stir the diet coke into the bowl. If the batter is very liquidy, don't worry!
  4. Bake for 25-35 minutes.
