Firecracker Ground Chicken

Description

Ground chicken cooked in chili oil and tossed with a sticky sweet and spicy firecracker sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1/2 tbsp chili oil
  • 1/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. Heat a skiller with chili oil. Brown both sides of the chicken (3-4 minutes per side).
  2. Combine other ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Once chicken is cooked, add mixture and cook until thick.
  4. Serve over rice.
