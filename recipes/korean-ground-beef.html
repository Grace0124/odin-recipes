Korean Ground Beef

Description

A simple, low calorie ground beef recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb extra lean ground beef
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 oz roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 2 tbsp gochujang
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp crushed red pepper

Directions

  1. Cook ground beef for 3-4 minutes, then add the roasted red peppers.
  2. Combine other ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add the mixture when almost no pink remains in the beef.
  4. Simmer for about 5 minutes.
  5. Serve over rice.
