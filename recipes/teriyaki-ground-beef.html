Teriyaki Ground-Beef Stir Fry

Description

Extra lean ground beef with zucchini, mushrooms, and a low calorie teriyaki sauce paired with a low carb rice blend and yum yum sauce to make meal prep bowls.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb extra lean ground beef
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 zucchinis, chopped
  • 6 oz sliced mushrooms
  • 2/3 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 5 cups cauliflower rice

Directions

  1. Brown the ground beef on a skiller with 1/2 tbsp of olive oil.
  2. Add remaining olive oil and zucchini. Cook for about 3 minutes.
  3. Add mushrooms and teriyaki suace. Cook about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Serve over rice. Garnish with scallions if desired.
