Cucumber, Onion, and Tomato Salad

Description

A crisp and refreshing side dish. Easy to make in advance and serve.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 cucumbers
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 onion

Directions

  1. Whisk dressing ingredients.
  2. Cut vegetables.
  3. Coat veggies in the dressing. Refrigerate 2 hours before serving.
